Welcome to the Life Journey with the Chovos Halevavos — a site dedicated to exploring the timeless teachings of Rabbeinu Bachya. Through shiurim, reflections, and guided learning, we aim to make the wisdom of the Chovos Halevavos practical and accessible in your daily spiritual journey.
