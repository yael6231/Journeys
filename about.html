About This Journey

Welcome to the Life Journey with the Chovos Halevavos. This site is dedicated to exploring the timeless wisdom of Rabbeinu Bahya ibn Paquda, whose teachings in Chovos Halevavos inspire inner growth and connection to Hashem.

Here, you'll find reflections, practical applications, and thoughtful discussion about the duties of the heart, bringing this foundational mussar work into our everyday lives.

Whether you're new to these ideas or have been learning them for years, you're invited to walk this journey together.
