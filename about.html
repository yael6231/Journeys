About This Blog

Welcome! This blog is a personal journey through the timeless and soul-nourishing teachings of Chovos Halevavos — “Duties of the Heart” — a classic work of Jewish ethics and inner life by Rabbi Bahya ibn Paquda.

Life is truly beautiful when lived with awareness of the heart's duties. The *Chovos Halevavos* opens our eyes to a world where trust in Hashem, humility, gratitude, and deep inner clarity become guiding lights in our daily choices.

This blog is a space for reflection, for slowing down, and for aligning life with purpose. Here is a place to share thoughts, insights, and struggles while trying to live each day with more meaning — through the lens of this sacred sefer.

If you've ever felt that there must be more to life than just going through the motions — you're not alone. The journey inward, guided by the *Chovos Halevavos*, reveals just how wonderful life can be when the heart leads with faith, wisdom, and connection to the Creator.

Thank you for being here. May our shared journey uplift and inspire.
